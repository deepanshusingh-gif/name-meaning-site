About My Name Finder

My Name Finder is a simple website created to help people explore the meanings of names from different cultures and languages in an easy way.

The information on this site is collected and generated from commonly known meanings, language patterns, and general numerology principles. It is meant for general interest and inspiration only, and should not be treated as professional, religious, or astrological advice.

Our goal is to keep improving the quality of name meanings over time and to add more names so that you can quickly look up meanings for yourself, your family, or future baby names.

If you notice any mistake in a name meaning, you can contact us and we will try to review and correct it.
